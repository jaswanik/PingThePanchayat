Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my complaint?
Log in to your account and visit the "My Complaints" section. Each complaint has a unique PTP-YEAR-XXXX reference number and a progress tracker showing Pending → In Progress → Resolved.
How long does resolution take?
High-severity issues (like water contamination or drainage overflow) are typically addressed within 24–48 hours. Medium and low severity issues may take 3–7 business days.
I forgot my password, what should I do?
Visit the login page and click "Forgot Password". You can reset your password using either a simulated OTP (sent to your registered mobile) or by answering your security question.
Can I submit multiple complaints?
Yes, you can submit as many complaints as needed. Each will receive a unique reference number and be tracked independently.
